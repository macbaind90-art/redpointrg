Industries We Serve

Every environment faces different pressures. We tune our scenarios, policies, and coaching to the realities of your sites and your people.

Healthcare & Behavioral Health

ED crowding, behavioral health holds, family conflict, elopement risks. We help align security, nursing, and administration so responses are safe, repeatable, and defensible.

Corporate Campuses

Visitor management, disgruntled employees, parking deck incidents, and after-hours coverage. We tighten playbooks and train officers to manage issues early and professionally.

Education

Focus on everyday reality: custody disputes, vaping, fights, trespassing, and threats. Scenarios reinforce communication with administration and law enforcement.

Industrial & Logistics Sites

Gate operations, contractor access, theft, and workplace violence risks. Training supports both production demands and safe, consistent enforcement.

How We Work

No canned program dropped on top of your campus. We follow a structured, repeatable process so training lines up with your actual risks and constraints.

  1. 1

    Assess

    We review your incidents, post orders, and layout. Short interviews with security, nursing, leadership, and frontline staff surface what is really happening.

  2. 2

    Design

    We build a focused package of policies, scenarios, and drills that fit your staffing, budget, and operational realities — not a generic “off-the-shelf” course.

  3. 3

    Train

    Instructor-led sessions, role-play, and scenario work on your campus or a suitable training site. Officers practice until responses are calm, consistent, and documentable.

  4. 4

    Validate

    Tabletop reviews, drills, and after-action debriefs confirm what is working and where we need to adjust policy, staffing, or training.

  5. 5

    Support

    Ongoing advisory support, refresher training, and help with incident trends so your program keeps pace with new risks and leadership expectations.
